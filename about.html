About Us

BindiPizza is not only proud to be Nairobi city Favorite Pizza, but also the leading pizza restaurant in Kenya. Our innovation and creativity has enabled us to offer the widest range of pizzas allowing our customers to choose from our range of classic, favorite, signature and something special pizzas. Our pizzas are made with thin crusts, lots of toppings, and the perfect blend of sauces.
